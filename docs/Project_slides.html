Data Science Semester Project

Smart Retail Demand Forecasting with Sales Decomposition and Cannibalization Analysis

Methodology & Architectural Decisions • Corporación Favorita

Section 1: Problem Context

Dual Failure Modes in Retail Inventory Management

  • Overstocking destroys capital and perishables.
  • Understocking causes severe revenue and customer loss.
  • Requires causal market physics, not black-boxes.

System Dimensionality and Forecasting Scope

  • 3.0 million daily transactional records processed.
  • 1,782 independent time-series vectors forecasted globally.
  • Multi-family, multi-store panel regression architecture.
3M Records ⨉ 54 Stores ⨉ 33 Families ⨉ 4.5 Years

The Governed Data Architecture Pipeline

  • Six sequential, rigidly governed execution phases.
  • Connects raw ingestion to SHAP auditing.
  • Every architectural node mapped to peer-reviewed literature.
Research Insight 1: Cerqueira et al.

The Illusion of Time-Series Cross-Validation

  • Standard validation protocols leak future data.
  • Inflates apparent performance by up to 20%.
  • Destroys integrity of autoregressive feature signals.
Application 1

The Impermeable Wall of Time

  • Enforced strict TimeSeriesSplit for validation.
  • Mandated prior shifts before rolling features.
  • Scalers fit exclusively on training rows.

Research Insight 3: Hewage et al.

The Complex Promotional Demand Lifecycle

  • Promotions create stock-piling, uplift, and post-dip.
  • Binary promo flags miss holistic temporal impacts.
  • True lift requires pre-promotion counterfactual baselines.

Research Insight 5: Ozaki et al.

Efficiency in Black-Box Optimization

  • Grid search is computationally prohibitive on ~3M rows.
  • Bayesian TPE samplers find optima 5x faster.
  • Enables multi-objective inventory cost optimization later.
Application 5

Scalable Bayesian Hyperparameter Tuning

  • Deployed Optuna for robust tree model tuning.
  • Searched complex parameter spaces within computation budget.
  • Ensured optimal model fit without data leakage.
